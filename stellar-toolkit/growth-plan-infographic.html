Two truths before the plan

Hackathons build credibility. Users and mainnet build valuation. You need both — but they are not the same thing.

01

Prizes ≠ valuation

Even five $10K hackathon wins do not make Stellar worth $1M. A priced seed round or convertible note does. Wins are proof points that earn the investor conversation.

02

Speed ≠ more hackathons

The path is: ship mainnet → activate Astroman list → prove retention → raise a small round. Hackathons feed proof. Real users feed valuation.

Winning formula: Mainnet launch + 200 beta users + 30% week-2 retention → $750K–$1.2M SAFE cap
