### Thread copy (paste-ready)

**1/10 — Hook**  
Most “consumer apps on Solana” are still built for people who already live on-chain.

We built for someone else: a person who spent $800 on a Dobsonian and just wants to know *what to point at tonight*.

No seed phrase. No gas talk. Email signup → embedded wallet → done.

That’s Stellar. 🧵

**2/10 — The insight judges miss**  
Consumer ≠ “mobile wallet.” Consumer = *activity context*.

Our users aren’t trading at a desk. They’re on a mountain with **no LTE** — Bortle 1–3, 2 hours from the city. Cloud AI dies exactly when they need help.

If your app only works online, it’s not consumer-ready for that category.

**3/10 — Two runtimes, one product**  
Same assistant (ASTRA), two homes:

• **Web** — planning at home: 7-day sky forecast, planet tracker, marketplace (OpenAI + real astronomy APIs)  
• **Stellar Field (Android)** — at the eyepiece: Llama 3.2 1B + Whisper **on-device** via Tether QVAC

User doesn’t pick “edge vs cloud.” The product picks for them.

**4/10 — Proof it’s real (not a slide deck)**  
Tether Frontier Hackathon track — **winner**, May 2026.

Shipped: APK, GitHub release, airplane-mode chat + voice log on a mid-range phone (Poco X3 NFC).

Demo: {{YOUTUBE_URL}}  
APK: https://github.com/Rezimod/Stellar/releases/download/v0.1.0-field/app-release.apk

**5/10 — What “invisible Solana” looks like**  
• Privy: email / Google → embedded Solana wallet auto-created  
• Gasless devnet txs (fee payer on server)  
• Verified sky photos → Metaplex Bubblegum cNFT (“Discovery Attestation”)  
• Stars SPL → redeemable toward **real telescopes** at astroman.ge

They learn they’re on-chain when we *show* them the proof — not at signup.

**6/10 — Distribution > demo day**  
Most crypto products: great demo, zero shelf.

We have Astroman — Georgia’s astronomy store, ~60K Facebook followers, physical showroom, real inventory.

Stellar isn’t “go viral on CT.” It’s “answer the question every buyer asks on night one.”

**7/10 — Consumer insight for builders**  
If you’re building “consumer on Solana,” ask:

1. Does it work in the user’s **worst** network conditions?  
2. Does onboarding avoid wallet jargon on day 0?  
3. Is there a **non-crypto** reason to open the app weekly? (For us: the sky changes every night.)  
4. Can rewards settle in something they already buy? (Telescopes, not points.)

**8/10 — What we deliberately didn’t ship first**  
No prediction-market skin. No “trending” theater. No gradient-degen UI.

Reference: NASA calm, not Kalshi noise. Utility first, crypto second.

(Markets exist on devnet — but the primary user is a telescope owner, not a trader.)

**9/10 — Edge AI lesson from Field**  
On-device isn’t a gimmick — it’s unit economics.

~20 AI chats/night × 5 nights ≈ 1,200 cloud calls/month/user. That’s margin leak for a retailer bundling software with hardware.

QVAC: one-time model download → marginal cost ≈ 0 at the dark-sky site.
