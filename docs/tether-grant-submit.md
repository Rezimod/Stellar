## Pitch rules for Tether reviewers

**Lead with:**
- Frontier track **winner** (127 submissions), public APK, airplane-mode proof  
- **QVAC where cloud fails** — dark-sky sites, zero LTE  
- **Astroman distribution** — real store, 60K audience, Georgian customers for NMT  

**Do not lead with:**
- Solana, prediction markets, Privy, cNFTs (mention only as “sync when online”)  
- QVAC infra bounties (Swift / CoreML / LTX) — wrong program  
- iOS (out of scope Phase 2)  

**One-liner:**  
*Frontier winner; flagship consumer app proving QVAC at the telescope with zero signal — distributed through a real astronomy retailer.*
